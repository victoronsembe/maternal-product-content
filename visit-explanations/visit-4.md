# Visit 4 (Around 20 Weeks): Anatomy Scan Review

## Purpose
To review the detailed results from your big anatomy scan ultrasound and check in on your baby's movements.

## What Happens
This visit is often filled with excitement as you discuss the detailed pictures of your baby's organs, development, and growth. Your provider will also ask about fetal movement, and you can discuss preparing for the second half of your pregnancy, like birth preparation classes.
